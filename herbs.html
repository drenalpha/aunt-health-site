Ethiopian Healing Herbs

Passed down from generation to generation, Ethiopia's herbs are used not only for flavor but also for healing. Here are five well-known herbs:

🌿 1. Tenadam (Rue)

  • Used to treat colds and headaches
  • Believed to ward off evil spirits
  • Often added to coffee for its aroma

🌱 2. Damakese

  • Helps with digestive issues
  • Strong antibacterial properties
  • Often used in soups and traditional medicine

🌿 3. Besobila (Ethiopian Sacred Basil)

  • Used in traditional stews (like Doro Wat)
  • Soothes the stomach
  • Believed to purify the blood

🌾 4. Tikur Azmud (Black Seed / Nigella Sativa)

  • Known as a “cure for everything but death”
  • Boosts immunity
  • Used for respiratory and digestive health

🌸 5. Kosso (Hagenia Abyssinica)

  • Traditional treatment for tapeworms and parasites
  • Flowers are dried and brewed into a medicinal drink
  • Must be used carefully — strong effects!

Ethiopia’s herbal traditions are still alive today — natural, powerful, and deeply cultural. 🌿
